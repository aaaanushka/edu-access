Equal Education, Digital Inclusion

We provide essential tools and platforms for students who lack access to quality digital education.

In the digital age, access to knowledge shouldn't be a privilege. EduAccess is here to ensure that even the most remote corners of the country have a fair chance at learning and growth.

Our Core Features

Offline Materials

Downloadable PDFs, animated lessons, and simple ebooks optimized for offline use in low-resource environments.

  • Class 6-12 NCERT content
  • Basic English learning modules
  • Science demos in local languages

Volunteer Tutors

Connect with real humans who care. Our volunteers offer support through SMS, WhatsApp, and voice notes for students with limited internet access.

  • Weekly live call sessions
  • Guidance on competitive exams
  • Subject-specific doubt clearing

Multi-language Support

All our content is translated and dubbed into various Indian languages to reach every learner in their comfort zone.

  • Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu
  • Voice-based navigation available
  • Localised examples and culture references
